Manage Money.
Together.

Econumo [eh-koh-NOO-mo] is a beautiful application for managing personal and family finances, supporting multiple currencies, joint accounts, and budgets, offering cloud-hosted or self-hosted options.

Get Started Demo

Key Features

  • Beautiful and Adaptive UI. Experience our elegant design that enhances usability, making it easy to manage finances whether you're at home, on the go, or at work. Enjoy a beautiful and responsive interface on mobile, tablet, or desktop in any modern browser.

  • Multi-User Support. Collaborate with family members or partners by managing finances together. Plan vacations, handle household expenses, or save for big purchases with ease, staying on the same page as a family or partnership.

  • Multi-Currency Support. Manage accounts in multiple currencies effortlessly, ideal for those who travel, live abroad, or have savings in different currencies.

  • Budgeting. Create and monitor budgets with ease to manage your everyday spending, plan for unforeseen expenses, save for goals such as buying a car, and tackle your debts.

  • API. Access your data through our API, allowing power users to create custom integrations and automate workflows.

  • Cloud-hosted or Self-hosted options. Enjoy control and flexibility over your financial management tools with the option to self-host the application for complete ownership.

  • Manual Transaction Entry. Keep your financial records accurate and up-to-date by manually entering transactions. This approach benefits those who like to have full control and want to develop a habit of tracking expenses.
