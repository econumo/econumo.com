## Accessing your user profile

You can access your profile details by clicking on your avatar in the left sidebar. Alternatively, go to Settings and click on your avatar there.

---
## Updating your user profile

You can update your name, avatar, or password.

**Note**  
Please note that changing your email is not currently supported in the user interface.

---
### How to change your email

For cloud customers, please contact [Support](mailto:econumo@gmail.com).

For self-hosted `Econumo`, please use a [CLI command](/docs/self-hosting/cli-commands/).

### How to change your avatar

Econumo pulls your avatar from [Gravatar](https://gravatar.com), linked to your email address. To change your avatar, please visit [Gravatar](https://gravatar.com).
