# Econumo apps

Econumo is a web application, but you can also use it as a regular mobile application on your iPhone, Android device, Mac, or PC.

---
## On iPhone:

1. Open the website [https://app.econumo.com](https://app.econumo.com) in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button (square with an upward arrow).
3. Select `Add to Home Screen` from the menu.
4. Rename the shortcut if desired, then tap `Add`. The web app will appear on your home screen and open in its own instance.

---
## On Android:

1. Open the website [https://app.econumo.com](https://app.econumo.com) in Google Chrome.
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
3. Select `Add to Home Screen` or `Install App` (if available).
4. Confirm by tapping `Add`. The web app will now appear on your home screen and function like an app.

---
## On Mac

1. Open the website [https://app.econumo.com](https://app.econumo.com) in Safari.
2. Click `File > Add to Dock` from the menu bar, or click the share icon in the Safari toolbar and select `Add to Dock`.
3. Confirm by clicking `Add`. The web app will appear in your Dock, Applications folder, and Launchpad for easy access.

---
## On PC

1. Open the website [https://app.econumo.com](https://app.econumo.com) in Microsoft Edge.
2. Click the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
3. Go to `Apps > Install this site as an app`.  
4. Confirm by clicking `Install`. The web app will be added to your Start menu and can also be pinned to your taskbar or desktop.
