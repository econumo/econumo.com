# v0.10.0

Published: January 25, 2026
URL: https://econumo.com/releases/v0.10.0/

The **v0.10.0** release raises the character limit for account names, categories, recipients, senders, tags, and budgets from 18 to 64 characters, giving you much more room for descriptive names.

The transaction list component has also been refined to prevent accidental switching.
