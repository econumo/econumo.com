# v0.3.0

Published: December 1, 2024
URL: https://econumo.com/releases/v0.3.0/

The **v0.3.0** release unifies Econumo's editions: the previously separate products are merged into a single, fully open-source **Econumo CE**, while the hosted service simply becomes **Econumo**.

## Changelog

- `Econumo Family` and `Econumo One` merged into `Econumo CE` and were fully open-sourced.
- `Econumo Cloud` became `Econumo`.
- Fixed a problem with currency rates for the Budget module.
